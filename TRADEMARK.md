# Nexa — trademark and distribution policy (community builds)

This document states **project policy** for the Nexa open-source repositories. It is **not** legal advice. Consult counsel for your jurisdiction.

## Names and branding

- **Nexa**, **Nexa Next**, and related marks identify products from this project’s maintainers.
- Third-party forks and distributions **must not** imply they are the official Nexa product, endorsed releases, or maintained by the core team unless explicitly authorized.

## Forks and derivatives **must not**:

- Use the official Nexa logo, wordmarks, or trade dress in a way that suggests endorsement by the core maintainers without permission.
- Label builds as **“Official Nexa”**, **“Nexa Official”**, or similar unless produced by the canonical release pipeline described in this repository’s documentation.
- Remove or obscure license and attribution notices required by **Apache-2.0** (or other applicable licenses).

## Acceptable use

- Describe forks accurately (for example: **“a fork of Nexa-next”**, **“based on Nexa”**).
- Ship under your own project name and branding while complying with the license.

## Registration

Trademark registration status varies by region and changes over time; **do not** infer registration from this file alone.

---

For branding questions or permission requests, open a discussion with repository maintainers.
