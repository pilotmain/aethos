# Commercial positioning — AethOS

## Why AethOS

AethOS is an **operational AI platform** with built-in Mission Control — not a chat wrapper or a fragmented agent stack.

## vs OpenClaw

- **Parity-first** execution and orchestration workflows
- **Mission Control** — Office, runtime truth, governance timeline, provider intelligence
- **Privacy posture** — operational egress and mode visibility
- **Differentiation** — local-first, brain-agnostic routing, workspace intelligence (see `docs/AETHOS_DIFFERENTIATION.md`)

## vs Paperclip-style stacks

- **Single runtime truth** — no duplicate dashboards
- **Lightweight workers** — ephemeral task-driven agents, not fake permanent personas
- **Plugin vs skill separation** — operational plugins vs AI execution skills

## Product pillars

1. **AethOS Orchestrator** — owns coordination
2. **Runtime Workers** — temporary, assignment-driven
3. **Providers** — Vercel and others with repair/deploy intelligence
4. **Brains** — multi-provider routing with confidence visibility
5. **Marketplace** — runtime plugins + skill marketplace
6. **Governance** — operational timeline, not raw logs
7. **Privacy Posture** — enforceable modes in production paths

## Commercial readiness (Phase 3 Step 5)

- Enterprise runtime confidence API
- Provider reliability summaries
- Onboarding readiness checks
- Lightweight cost estimates
- Team-oriented ownership clarity (no heavy RBAC)

## Enterprise operational surfaces (Phase 4 Step 9)

Fortune-500-caliber Mission Control readability without monitoring-dashboard bloat:

- Governance experience and timeline storytelling
- Executive operational overview and runtime narrative engine
- Worker ecosystem experience and unified explainability center
- Enterprise language system for calm, premium operator tone

## Enterprise identity and onboarding (Phase 4 Step 10)

- One-curl enterprise installer with doctor, validate, and orchestrator onboarding
- Adaptive provider routing visibility and unified restart discipline
- AethOS branding lock — user-facing coherence without breaking legacy env aliases

## Release candidate (Phase 4 Step 14)

- Enterprise release candidate certification and operational freeze
- Fortune-500-caliber Mission Control calmness under cold hydration
- `aethos runtime certify` — full RC certification bundle

## Philosophy

Lighter than enterprise orchestration stacks, cleaner than fragmented AI control planes, more transparent than opaque agents.
