# Enterprise explainability (final)

**Truth key:** `enterprise_explainability_final`  
**Center:** `runtime_explainability_center`

Operators can understand without logs:

- Why routing / fallback occurred
- Why degradation or recovery is active
- Why advisories and recommendations exist
- What orchestrator, workers, and providers are doing

APIs: `GET /api/v1/mission-control/explainability`, routing explanations on `/api/v1/runtime/routing/explanations`.
