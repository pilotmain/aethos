# Enterprise operational discipline

Under pressure, AethOS:

1. Prioritizes critical visibility (Office, recovery, orchestrator)
2. Defers low-priority intelligence slices
3. Preserves operator continuity and calm copy
4. Maintains advisory-first routing

Metrics on truth: `enterprise_operational_discipline` (pressure level, prioritization effectiveness, deferred workload, continuity and recovery scores).

See also [RUNTIME_PRESSURE_BEHAVIOR.md](RUNTIME_PRESSURE_BEHAVIOR.md).
