## Privacy

AethOS does not automatically “phone home” with your chat content. **What leaves your deployment** depends on **your** configuration (for example LLM API keys, Telegram, webhooks, telemetry you enable, and third-party services you connect). When you use third-party APIs (e.g. Anthropic, Open‑AI), **their** privacy policies and data processing terms apply to traffic you send them.

Review your `.env`, network egress settings, and provider dashboards for your actual data flows.
